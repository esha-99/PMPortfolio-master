Pasta Dough

Prep Time

5 mins

Total Time

30 mins

Yield

6 servings

Ingredients

Pasta Dough
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour sifted
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup water *start with 2 tablespoons

INSTRUCTIONS

Making the Pasta Dough:

1. In the stand mixer bowl, place the eggs, flour, salt and 2 tablespoons of water. Using the flat beater, attach it to the bowl and mix the ingredients on speed 2 for 30 seconds.

2. Remove the flat beater and replace it with the dough hook. Turn the dough on to speed 2 and let it knead for 2 minutes. At this time if the mixture looks too dry add 1 tablespoon of water at a time.

3. Remove the dough from the bowl and knead with your hands for 1-2 minutes. Form the dough into a ball, place plastic wrap over the top of the dough, and let it rest for 20 minutes.

Spaghetti Noodles:

1. Divide the dough into 4 pieces.

2. Attach the Pasta Sheet Roller attachment to you stand mixer.

3. Take 1 of the 4 pieces of dough and flatten it until its about a half-inch thick.

4. Set the Pasta Sheet Roller to setting 1 and your stand mixer to Speed 2/4 and begin to carefully feed the dough through the attachment.

5. Once this is done you'll want to fold the dough in half and then half again.

6. Make sure it fits in the top of the attachment. Then run it through the Pasta Sheet Roller again.

7. Next, move your Pasta Sheet Roller to setting 2 and then carefully run the dough through. Repeat this moving the setting all the way up to 5 until you have a thin sheet of pasta. I typically cut the pasta sheet in half by setting 2 or 3-it is much easier to work with.

8. Once you have 2 sheets of pasta, remove the Pasta Sheet Roller and add the Spaghetti attachment to the stand mixer.

9. Place the mixer on Speed 2 and carefully begin to feed the sheet of pasta through the attachment.

10. Once you have spaghetti twirl it into a "nest". Repeat these steps until all of your dough has been transformed into spaghetti.

Cook the Spaghetti:

Cook the spaghetti in a pot of boiling water with 1 tablespoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of olive oil for 3-4 minutes or until al dente
