Chicken Soup

Prep Time

30 mins

Total Time

3 hours

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 whole Chicken breast
  • 1 Onion
  • 6 cloves of Garlic
  • 2 turnips washed and grated or chopped
  • 3 carrots sliced
  • 3 potatoes cut in pieces
  • 2 zucchini sliced
  • 2 celery sliced
  • 2 table spoons of pearlbarley
  • 1 bunch of parsley chopped
  • 1 bag Egg Soup noodle

INSTRUCTIONS

Soup

1) Add chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, and barley into a large dutch oven

2) Cook chicken almost all the way

3) Add rest of the ingredients EXCEPT noodles

4) Put on a simmer for 3-4 hours.

5) Add salt, pepper, lemon juice to taste

6) 10 minutes before serving add noodles and cook for 10 minutes
