Popeyes Chicken Sandwhich

Prep Time

1 Hour

Total Time

3 hours

Yield

6 Sandwhiches

Ingredients

Japenese Milk Bun (Tangzhong)
  • 2 tablespoons (20g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons (27g) water
  • 4 tablespoons (60g) whole milk
Dough:
  • 1/2 cup(120g) whole milk @ 95f (30c)
  • 1 tablespoons (9g) instant yeast 2.5 cups (320g) Bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon (7g) fine sea salt
  • 2.5 tablespoons (35g) granulated sugar
  • 1 whole egg
  • egg yolk
  • tablespoons (42g) unsalted butter, softened
Egg wash:
  • 1 whole egg splash of whole milk
Chicken Marinade (Overnight):
  • 1/2 cup(120g) whole milk @ 95f (30c)
  • 1 tablespoons (9g) instant yeast 2.5 cups (320g) Bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon (7g) fine sea salt
  • 2.5 tablespoons (35g) granulated sugar
  • 1 whole egg
  • egg yolk
  • tablespoons (42g) unsalted butter, softened
Mayo Aioli:
  • 3/4 cup mayo
  • 8 cloves black garlic puree
  • 1 tbsp (16g) hot sauce
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • 1tsp (5g) smoked paprika
  • Lemon juice to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Milk Bun

1. Combine 60g whole milk + 2tbs water + 2tbs bread flour

2. Wisk together

3. Put over medium heat and whisk until it becomes gelation and put to side

4. Take half a cup whole milk and heat to 95 Degree F

5. Mix in 1 tbs instant yeast

6. Let it sit for 8 min

7. Combine in stand mixer 2 1/2 cups bread flour + 2.5 sugar + 1 tsp sea salt

8. Add milk yeast mixture let it run for a couple seconds

9. Stand mixer low speed add the dry mixture then add tazong + 1 whole egg + 1 egg yolk (room temperature)

10. Scrape down side intermittenly

11. Add 3 tablespoons unsalted softened butter not all at once

12. knead dough for 5-8 min

13. Take dough out and put into a ball and make smooth

14. Put in a grease bowl and cover with damp bowl and let it rest for 1-1.5 hours or until double in size can put in turned off oven with light on

15. Take dough and punch it down

16. Light flour workspace

17. Divide dough to 6 even pieces (95g-105g each)

18. Shape the pieces and fold into itself then rotate the dough

19. Repeat with all the doughs

20. Put on greased baking sheet with paper on bottom (space them apart)

21. Put another baking sheet on top and let it rise in room temperature for 1-2hours or double in size

22. Make egg wash (1 whole egg + splash water) and brush the doughs

23. Bake 375 F for 16-18min until brown

Chicken Marinade:

1. Add chicken to marinade

2. Let it sit 3 hours minimum, preferrably overnight

3. Once marinated, grab a dutch oven and fill with neutral oil

4. Heat to 350 Degrees

5. Add droplets of marinade into flour mixture

6. Coat chicken in flour and place on baking sheet

7. Cook chicken in oil until 165 Degrees F

8. Assemble with buns, chicken, aioli, pickle
