Potato Crisp w/ Aioli

Prep Time

10 mins

Total Time

45 mins

Yield

6 servings

Ingredients

Potato
  • 1 bag of small colored potatoes
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp oniown powder
  • 1/2 tsp cajun
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Aioli
  • Aioli
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/2 tbsp
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp paprika
  • 1/4 tbsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • salt/pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Potatoes:

1. Boil potatoes with kosher salt for 15min until tender

2. Throw potatoes in smash potatoes down Bake 400 f for 30 min each side

Aioli:

1. Mix all ingredients for aioli
